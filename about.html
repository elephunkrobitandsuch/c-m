Wait.... Dinosaurs?

I’m in L.A. for the same reason we all are: DINOSAURS. At around the age of 13, after repeat viewings of Jurassic Park on laser disc, I decided that I would become a paleontologist…

But, it turns out that takes a lot of school, and I wasn’t too fond of school (musical theater and Star Trek wasn’t “in” in the 90s). Also paleontologists don’t get to meet living dinosaurs like they do in the movies, so….

From there I realized the dream beneath the dream: to one day pretend to be a paleontologist, spaceship captain, and beyond!

But it gets BETTER. I’ve met some really incredible people in Los Angeles, and found joy in offering support and expertise to help them make their stories a reality. Finding fulfillment in front of and behind the camera has been an exciting journey.
